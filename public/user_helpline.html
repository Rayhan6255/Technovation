Frequently Asked Questions

How can I pay my fines online?

You can pay fines directly from the "My Fines" section using a mobile banking service like bKash or Nagad, or with a credit/debit card.

What documents do I need for my vehicle?

You must carry your valid driving license, vehicle registration certificate, tax token, and fitness certificate at all times.

How do I get a Clearance Certificate?

A Clearance Certificate confirms that you have no pending traffic violations. You can apply for one from the "Clearance Certificate" section of this portal.
